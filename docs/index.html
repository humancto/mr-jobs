Features

Everything to automate your job search

7+ Discovery Sources

Greenhouse, Lever, Indeed, LinkedIn, Glassdoor, RemoteOK, HN Who's Hiring, and custom career pages.

AI Resume Scoring

Every job scored 0-100 against your actual resume, skills, and career goals. Deep semantic analysis, not keyword matching.

Resume Tailoring

AI generates tailored summaries, achievement bullets, and cover letters for each job posting.

Browser Automation

Playwright fills application forms automatically. Dry-run mode for safety. Works with any ATS.

Smart Tracking

Follow-up reminders auto-set on apply. Ghost detection flags no-response after 14 days. Nothing slips through.

Pluggable AI

Claude CLI default (free with Pro). Swap in OpenAI, Ollama, or any LLM. Different models per component.
